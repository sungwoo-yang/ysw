## Game Introduction
OLLIM is based on the concept of a celestial spirit who embarks on a journey to rescue the 12 constellations that have fallen for unknown reasons. Traverse a vast map, solve puzzles, and reach boss chambers where you must complete light-reflection puzzles to save each constellation.

## Game Play Guide
- Starting Map: A tutorial for movement. Read the signs and use jump and dash to reach the door.
- Second Map: A showcase of the game's star systems. Read the signs to learn how each star system works.
- Final Map: The boss stage. You can get a quick preview of how boss fights will progress.


##  Controls

- Left / Right Movement: A (left), D (right)
- Jump: W or Space
- Interaction: F (read signboards, activate campfires, etc.)
- Dash: LShift


# Stars and Light

Stars: There are “light-emitting stars” and “dead stars” that require light in the game world.

Light Emission: Light-emitting stars fire two types of light at fixed intervals.

⚪ White Light: Deals no damage and can be reflected with the shield.

🟡 Yellow Light: Deals minor damage and can be reflected with the shield.

🔴 Red Light: Deals near-fatal damage upon impact. It cannot be simply reflected, but it can be parried using the shield. (However, parrying is impossible under certain conditions.)



# Light Puzzle System

Shield Creation: Hold the right mouse button (Right-click) to create a shield at your mouse cursor's position.

Slow Movement: While holding the shield (holding Right-click), the player's movement speed decreases.

Light Reflection & Parrying:

You can reflect light using your shield.

The reflected light can be used to reactivate "dead stars" or trigger various puzzle mechanisms throughout the game.

Certain light can only be blocked by parrying. To successfully parry, you must block with your shield right before the light is fired.


# Full Gameplay Guide

Since the in-game tutorials are currently incomplete, this guide provides a comprehensive overview of how to play.

Getting Started & Movement
Initialization: When you click "Start Game," your character will fall to the starting area.

Learning the Basics: You can press [F] to read the instructional signs scattered throughout the area. These will guide you through the basic movement controls.

Progression: Follow the instructions while heading toward the brown door.

Entering the Map: Interacting with the door will trigger a brief animation and transport you to the next map.

Navigating the Stage
Objective: Once on the new map, use your jump skills to navigate toward the visible door to trigger the boss fight.

Recovery: If you fall off the map, press [R] to respawn at your last save point.

Boss Fight Mechanics
1. The boss battle consists of the following sequence:

2. The Chase: Start from the left and navigate through various obstacles and puzzles to reach the far right. A laser will continuously pursue you throughout this section.

3. Transition: Reach the door at the far right and interact with it before the laser catches you to move to the Constellation Gimmick area.

4. The Puzzle: Identify the brightest star and reflect the light it emits to activate another star.

Victory Condition: Repeat this process until all stars in the constellation are lit.

Note on Current Development:
Currently, only Phase 1 is fully implemented. For Phase 2 and Phase 3, you can simply proceed to the door and interact with it to skip directly to the boss patterns. Once the boss is defeated, a final door will appear, allowing you to exit. Currently, only one boss is available for play.
